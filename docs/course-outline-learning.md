# Course Outline (Learning)

This outline is for learning.  
It is not a project spec.  

## Course goals
You will build a Bitcoin wallet app for Android.  
You will learn Android basics first.  
You will learn Bitcoin and security later.  

## Course shape
The course has 7 parts.  
The course runs for 12 weeks.  
Each week has lessons and a quiz.  
Each part ends with a summary.  
Each part has next steps.  

## Part 1: Foundation
Week 1 covers setup and tools.  
Week 1 introduces Java basics.  
Week 2 covers app structure.  
Week 2 introduces simple UI.  

## Part 2: Android Core
Week 3 covers activities and navigation.  
Week 3 covers storage basics.  
Week 4 covers networking.  
Week 4 covers data flow.  

## Part 3: Cryptography
Week 5 covers hashing and keys.  
Week 6 covers HD wallets and mnemonics.  

## Part 4: Bitcoin Core
Week 7 covers transactions and scripts.  
Week 8 covers fees and broadcasting.  

## Part 5: Security
Week 9 covers secure storage.  
Week 9 covers biometrics.  
Week 10 covers threats.  
Week 10 covers hardening.  

## Part 6: Reproducible Builds
Week 11 covers deterministic builds.  
Week 11 covers verification.  

## Part 7: Final App
Week 12 covers final polish.  
Week 12 covers release planning.  
Part 7 has a short history of Android wallet stacks.
